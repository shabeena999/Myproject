ABOUT US

A vehicle rental, hire vehicle or vehicle hire agency is a company that rents automobiles for short periods of time to the public, generally ranging from a few hours to a few weeks. It is often organized with numerous local branches (which allow a user to return a vehicle to a different location), and primarily located near airports or busy city areas and often complemented by a website allowing online reservations. Vehicle rental agencies primarily serve people who require a temporary vehicle, for example, those who do not own their own vehicle, travelers who are out of town, or owners of damaged or destroyed vehicles who are awaiting repair or insurance compensation.
